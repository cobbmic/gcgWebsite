Help Panama City Beach

For decades, Panama City Beach has hosted Gulf Coast Getaway. This community has given so much to our students and ministries over the years. They have patiently served us in their restaurants and stores, and the Edgewater staff have welcomed us to their property each year.

This weekend, we are asking you to give back to this community. The donations we collect this weekend will go to two efforts. Some of the donations will go to a family who lost their home and more to Hurricane Michael. Autrell has worked at the Edgewater for years. His family's home was destroyed and many of their belongings lost. Some of our donations will go towards their efforts to recover from the devastation. The rest of the donations will go to the Distaster Response Team to assist their efforts in cleaning up and rebuilding in the aftermath of Hurricane Michael.
